# Requirements: Update Helix License Terms

## Overview

Update the Helix license and README to clarify that free licenses for small businesses are not an entitlement but a discretionary offering that may be modified or discontinued.

## User Stories

### As HelixML leadership
- I want the license to clearly indicate that small business free use is a privilege, not a right
- I want to communicate to users that the free tier policy has changed without being adversarial

### As a prospective user
- I want to understand the current licensing terms clearly
- I want to know that I need to check current pricing if I'm a commercial user

## Acceptance Criteria

### License (LICENSE.md)
- [ ] Modify Section 4.2 wording around "Small Business Environment" to indicate this is subject to change and not guaranteed
- [ ] Add language clarifying that free tier availability is at Helix's sole discretion
- [ ] Keep changes subtle and professional in tone
- [ ] Update the "Last updated" date

### README (README.md)
- [ ] Update the License section to remove explicit mention of "free" small business use
- [ ] Reword to direct users to pricing page for current terms
- [ ] Maintain friendly, professional tone
- [ ] Keep the section concise

## Out of Scope
- Pricing page changes (separate system)
- Changes to for-mac/LICENSE (proprietary, separate terms)
- Email notifications to existing users
