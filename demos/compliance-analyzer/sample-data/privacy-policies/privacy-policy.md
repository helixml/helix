# Privacy Policy — TechFlow Solutions Ltd

**Last updated: 15 January 2026**

## 1. Who We Are

TechFlow Solutions Ltd ("TechFlow", "we", "us", "our") is a software-as-a-service company registered in England and Wales (Company No. 12345678), with our registered office at 42 Innovation Way, London, EC2A 1NX, United Kingdom.

We are the data controller for the personal data described in this policy. If you have any questions about how we handle your data, you can contact us at privacy@techflow.io or write to our registered address above.

Our Data Protection Officer is Sarah Chen, who can be contacted at dpo@techflow.io.

## 2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

**Account Information**: Name, email address, company name, job title, and phone number when you create a TechFlow account.

**Payment Data**: Credit card details and billing address, processed by our payment provider Stripe. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

**Usage Data**: Information about how you use our platform, including features accessed, pages visited, actions taken, timestamps, and session duration.

**Device and Technical Data**: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, and referring URLs.

**Communications Data**: Emails, support tickets, and chat messages you send to us, including any attachments.

**Data You Upload**: Files, documents, and other content you upload to the TechFlow platform as part of using our services.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

- **To provide our services**: Processing your account registration, managing your subscription, and delivering the features of the TechFlow platform. (Legal basis: performance of our contract with you.)
- **To process payments**: Charging your payment method for subscriptions and handling billing queries. (Legal basis: performance of our contract with you.)
- **To send service communications**: Sending transactional emails about your account, including password resets, subscription confirmations, and important product updates. (Legal basis: performance of our contract with you.)
- **To send marketing emails**: Sending newsletters, product announcements, and promotional offers. (Legal basis: your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.)
- **To improve our services**: Analysing usage patterns to understand how our platform is used and identify areas for improvement. (Legal basis: our legitimate interest in improving our products.)
- **To prevent fraud**: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity and abuse of our platform. (Legal basis: our legitimate interest in protecting our business and users.)
- **To comply with legal obligations**: Retaining certain records as required by UK tax and financial regulations. (Legal basis: compliance with legal obligations.)

## 4. Marketing and Consent

We will only send you marketing communications if you have opted in by ticking the marketing consent checkbox during registration or in your account settings.

You can withdraw your consent to marketing at any time by:
- Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email
- Updating your preferences in your account settings under Communications
- Emailing us at privacy@techflow.io

Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of any processing we carried out before you withdrew. After you unsubscribe, we will stop sending marketing emails within 48 hours, though you will continue to receive essential service communications.

## 5. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your personal data with the following categories of recipients:

- **Stripe** (payment processing) — processes your payment card data under their own privacy policy
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** (cloud hosting) — hosts our platform infrastructure in the EU (Ireland region)
- **Google Analytics** (usage analytics) — helps us understand how our platform is used
- **Intercom** (customer support) — powers our in-app chat and help desk
- **SendGrid** (email delivery) — sends transactional and marketing emails on our behalf
- **HubSpot** (CRM) — manages our sales pipeline and customer relationships

All our third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements and are required to process your data only on our instructions.

## 6. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers (including Google, Intercom, and SendGrid) are based in the United States. When your data is transferred outside the UK and European Economic Area, we ensure adequate protection through:

- The UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA) or EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
- Where applicable, reliance on adequacy decisions by the UK Secretary of State or the European Commission

You can request a copy of the relevant transfer safeguards by contacting us at privacy@techflow.io.

## 7. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account information | Duration of your account plus 2 years after deletion |
| Payment records | 7 years after the transaction (UK tax requirements) |
| Usage and analytics data | 26 months from collection |
| Support correspondence | 3 years from ticket closure |
| Marketing preferences | Until you unsubscribe or close your account |
| Data you upload to the platform | Deleted within 30 days of account closure |

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymise it.

## 8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access** — You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days and provide the first copy free of charge.
- **Right to rectification** — You can ask us to correct inaccurate data or complete incomplete data. You can also update most information directly in your account settings.
- **Right to erasure** — You can ask us to delete your personal data. We will comply unless we have a legal obligation to retain it (e.g., financial records for tax purposes).
- **Right to restriction** — You can ask us to restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances, such as while we verify the accuracy of your data.
- **Right to data portability** — You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON or CSV). Where technically feasible, we can transfer it directly to another service provider.
- **Right to object** — You can object to processing based on our legitimate interests. You have an absolute right to opt out of direct marketing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@techflow.io or write to our registered address. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

## 9. Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organisational measures, including:

- Encryption of data at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Multi-factor authentication for all employee access to production systems
- Regular penetration testing and vulnerability scanning
- Access controls based on the principle of least privilege
- Annual security awareness training for all staff
- Continuous monitoring and alerting for suspicious activity

## 10. Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a personal data breach, we will:

- Notify the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, where the breach is likely to result in a risk to individuals' rights and freedoms
- Notify affected individuals without undue delay if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms
- Document the breach, its effects, and the remedial actions taken
